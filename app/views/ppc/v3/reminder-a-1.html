How would you like your prepayment certificate sent?

Information We do not send out plastic cards any more because they take time to reach you. They are also costly to the NHS and the environment

We can send your prepayment certificate straight away by email. Or, you can write down or print your details at the end of the service.

You will get a:

  • certificate number
  • start date
  • end date

If you choose to receive your certificate by email you will also receive an email reminder a month before your certificate runs out.

If you choose to write down your certificate details you will not receive a reminder.
