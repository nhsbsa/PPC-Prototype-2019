When do you want your prepayment certificate to start?


You can choose any date between 15 July 2018 and 15 September 2018.

If you are renewing your certificate please choose a start date after the expiry date of your current certificate.

I have already collected my prescription

If you have already collected and paid for your prescription, you can claim a refund if you received a FP57 form from the pharmacy.

To do this you should select the start date to be the date in the past on which you collected your prescription.

For example, 1 9 2018
